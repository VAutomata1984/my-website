UI & Web Design

Since graduating college, I've worked as a freelance web and UI designer. I've collaborated with a diverse array of clients, from small businesses to startups, in order to craft modern, visually appealing promotional and professional websites. I work hard to ensure that the websites I create for my clients both properly express their brand identity and reach as wide of an audience as possible.

My creative process first involves wireframing and making mockups in Figma. I then create the actual websites either by coding them from hand using HTML/CSS and JavaScript, or by using a CMS of a client's choice (Wix, Squarespace, Wordpress, etc.).

Some of my previous clients include: JOP Bookkeeping and Taxes, Modern Tibet, AVO Electric, Local 10 Roofers and Waterproofers and many more.

You can find my full visual portfolio at Behance.

To see my web design works specifically, check out my profile on Dribbble.
