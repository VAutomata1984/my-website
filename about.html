About Me

My whole life, I've been fascinated by the stories people tell. This interest in both people, and the technologies they use to communicate with one another, led me to pursue a BA in Computer Science at Columbia University. At university, I not only studied enriching contemporary topics such as AI, NLP, and computer vision, but I also investigated how exactly people interact with these nascent technologies and how this interaction shapes society at large. My studies have also led me to explore a wide range of subjects outside of computer science, including: neurolinguistics, sociolinguistics, philosophy and sociology.

In my work, my main goal is to utilize digital mediums in order to foster connections between people. Whether it's giving clients an outlet to share their work with the world, or helping a small business get their foot in the digital door, I am passionate about connecting people and ensuring their work is well designed and visible to a wider audience.

For the past couple months, I've been working as a freelance web/UI designer, ensuring my clients have a sleek and modern-looking website thay they can use to connect with and share their vision with others. If you would like to see more of my work, please look at my portfolio. From there, you can chart both my educational and professional journey and see firsthand the various ways that I've helped people to express themselves and their visions.

In addition to helping clients tell their stories, I am passionate about telling some of my own. Out of the many mediums I've used to express myself, the one I am most passionite about is writing. As my main form of personal and artistic expression, I try to write as frequently as I can about a wide variety of topics ranging from short fiction stories to articles about contemporary society.



You can visit my Medium where I write about numerous topics that interest me, ranging from technology to art to music.

Feel free to explore my visual portfolio on Behance, as well as my more technical projects on Github.

If you would like inquire more about my web/UI/UX design services, please feel free to reach out to me directly.
