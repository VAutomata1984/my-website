About Me...

Hi! My name's Matt. I graduated with a BA in Computer Science from Columbia University. I was born and raised in Queens, NY.

Storytelling has always been my passion. In my professional carreer, I seek to combine that passion with my interest in computer science and tech. Utilizing my diverse skillset and myriad interests, my main goal is to foster communication and connection between people. This passion has led me to acquire experiences working with Writing, Designing, Programming, Creative Coding and Art. Both personal and client work in these areas can be found in my portfolio.
